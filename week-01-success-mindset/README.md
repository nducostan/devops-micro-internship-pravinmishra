# Assignment 1. What is something you believe to be true that most people around you would disagree with?

### Rules

* No "safe" answers.
* Must be your real belief (not copied from internet).
* Minimum 50 words.

**Hint:** What do you believe about career, money, learning, discipline, relationships, health, success, life, tech industry, etc. that most people don't agree with?

## Answer

My belief:
I believe that I can learn and understand virtually anything, no matter how difficult it seems at first, provided I apply enough consistency, patience, and hard work. Most people tend to think that intelligence or natural talent sets a fixed limit on what a person can achieve. I disagree. While talent may help someone learn faster, I believe persistence is far more powerful in the long run. Given enough time, deliberate practice, and the willingness to keep going through failures and confusion, I believe almost any skill or subject can be mastered. The biggest limitation is often not intelligence, but the decision to give up too soon.
